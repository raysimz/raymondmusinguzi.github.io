Why RentEase is Different

A rental platform built on trust, transparency, and technology

The Problem with Traditional Rental Sites

Most classifieds list landlord contacts for free – attracting scammers, fake ads, and wasted time. Tenants end up calling dozens of numbers only to find properties already rented or non‑existent.

The RentEase Innovation

Payment‑gated contact unlock – Tenants pay a small fee (UGX 5,000) to reveal the landlord’s phone, WhatsApp, and location map. This simple filter eliminates fake inquiries and ensures only serious tenants connect.

Real Impact

Since launch, RentEase has reduced rental scams by over 60% in pilot areas. Landlords post for free, get verified tenants, and manage their listings through a dashboard. Tenants save time and money, and every payment is tracked.

What Makes RentEase Different

  • ✅ Landlords pay zero commission – forever
  • ✅ Tenants pay only to unlock contact – no hidden fees
  • ✅ Built‑in chat, reviews, and fraud detection
  • ✅ Google Maps location picker for exact property position
  • ✅ Email OTP verification and password reset
  • ✅ Admin dashboard for oversight and verification requests
