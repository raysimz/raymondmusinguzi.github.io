About Raymond Musinguzi

Visionary tech entrepreneur driving digital and structural innovation

From Uganda to Global Tech & Architecture Innovation

Raymond Musinguzi is a Ugandan tech entrepreneur, software developer, and architecture engineer who combines technical expertise with structural design knowledge to create impactful digital and physical solutions. As the founder and technical lead of Raysimz, he leads an international team delivering cutting-edge software, robotics training, architecture planning, and digital transformation services.

His journey in technology and engineering began during his early education in Uganda, where he developed a passion for solving real-world problems through code, innovation, and structural design. Today, Raymond Musinguzi is recognized for his hands-on approach to software architecture, his expertise in construction planning, his commitment to STEM education through robotics training, and his ability to bridge the gap between technology, architecture, and practical business applications.

Beyond technical development, Raymond Musinguzi applies his engineering skills to architecture and construction projects, designing residential complexes and estate layouts while maintaining his involvement in event hosting, digital branding initiatives, bringing technology, creativity, and youth empowerment together to foster the next generation of African innovators.
